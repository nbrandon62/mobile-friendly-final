Welcome to REFLOW

Our Mission

"To donate to REFLOW is a step towards sustainability"

We are focused on recyling and upcycling clothes here at REFLOW. Every piece of clothes donated to REFLOW is another piece of clothing in the market that doesn't need to be made. We curate the out favorite pieces from our donation centers and personally donate the rest of the pieces to other thrift stores. This follows through entirely the ethos of REFLOW. Better purchasing, better planet.

How To Donate

"Better purchases, better planet."

We've teamed up with Recology here in the Bay Area to work out of the existing donation centers you might see in a strip mall. You know, the ones with the open and close mechanism and it looks like a very large mailbox. Drop off whatever clothing donations you have there. We have dedicated teams here at REFLOW to sift through your clothes and pass them onto to the next person. This leads to better purchases for a better planet.
